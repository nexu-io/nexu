# One-Minute Quick Start

Download nexu, connect a channel, and your AI Agent is ready to go.

## Step 1: Launch nexu

1. [Download](https://nexu.io/download) and install the nexu desktop app.
2. Open it, sign in or create an account.

## Step 2: Connect a channel

Pick one channel and follow the setup guide:

- [Feishu](/guide/channels/feishu) — only need App ID + App Secret
- [Slack](/guide/channels/slack) — one-click app creation via manifest
- [Discord](/guide/channels/discord) — need Application ID + Bot Token

Enter your credentials and click "Connect".

## Step 3: Start chatting

Once the channel shows "Connected", click "Chat" to jump to your chat platform and send a message to your bot.

That's it! Your AI Agent is live 🎉
